# Product

## Register

product

## Users

College students who are trying to stay on top of assignments, classes, study sessions, and campus study groups. They may be using the app between classes, while studying in the library, or when they feel behind and need a clear next step.

## Product Purpose

AntiProcrastination helps students turn schoolwork into manageable focus sessions, connect with classmates for study support, and build motivation through streaks, badges, coins, and shared study rooms. Success means students quickly understand what to work on next, feel less overwhelmed, and can find people to study with when they need help.

## Brand Personality

Social, motivating, and friendly. The app should feel supportive and student-centered, with enough personality to make studying feel less lonely while still respecting that students are here to get work done.

## Anti-references

Avoid corporate productivity software, childish gamification, crowded dashboards, and boring school portal patterns. The app should not feel like an admin system, a generic calendar clone, or a cluttered social feed.

## Design Principles

1. Make the next step obvious.
2. Keep social features lightweight and useful.
3. Reward progress without distracting from studying.
4. Let the selected school identity personalize the experience.
5. Reduce overwhelm before adding more capability.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use clear contrast, simple language, and large touch targets. Support reduced-motion preferences and avoid relying on color alone to communicate important state.
